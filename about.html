About Me

I am Om Patil, a passionate Software Developer with a Master of Science in Computer Science from California State University, Chico, holding a GPA of 3.1. My journey in tech began with a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Engineering from Vishwakarma Institute of Technology, where I graduated with a GPA of 3.04.

My professional experience includes internships at 7Quebs and Extentia Information Technology, where I honed my skills in full-stack and front-end development, respectively. I excel in technologies such as React, Angular, Docker, and Git, and have a keen interest in developing scalable web applications.

I've led and contributed to projects that leverage modern web technologies, focusing on creating seamless user experiences and optimizing performance. I am always eager to learn new technologies and methodologies that enhance software development and deployment processes.

In my free time, I enjoy contributing to open-source projects and staying updated with the latest trends in software development. I'm also passionate about using technology to solve real-world problems, making an impact one line of code at a time.
